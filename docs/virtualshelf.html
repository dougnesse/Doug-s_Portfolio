Artificial Intelligence in the 1980s

I chose the Artificial Intelligence section of the library because the vintage look of the books caught my eye. The collection from the 1980s made me curious about early views on AI, and this shelf held a strong variety of books on this topic.

Timeline of Publications

The timeline reveals that most books were published between 1985 and 1989. This shows a clear cluster during this period and a noticeable absence outside it, reflecting a peak of interest in AI research during those years (at least in books).

Network & Map Visualizations

The network shows limited direct co-authorship, but shared themes like natural language processing and instructional use of AI link many works indirectly.

The map shows publication centers in Chichester, Amsterdam, Reading, New York, and Indianapolis, indicating major international interest in AI literature at the time.
