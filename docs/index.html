Football Explaination

American football is a team sport where two teams of 11 players each aim to score points by advancing an oval-shaped football into the opposing team's end zone. Teams can move the ball by running with it or passing it. The game is played on a 100-yard field with goalposts at each end. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.This bulleted list contains some American football terms.

  • Fumble
  • Interception
  • Safety
  • Sack
  • Touchback
  • Onside Kick
  • Punt
