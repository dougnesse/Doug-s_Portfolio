Document Collections

This text analysis project uses inaugural speeches by Joe Biden and Donald Trump. The goal is to compare their verbal styles and themes using text analysis tools like Voyant and AntConc. The Biden speech generally emphasizes unity and democracy, while the Trump speech focuses on national renewal and power.

Joe Biden Analysis

In Biden's address, frequent phrases like "my whole soul" and "we must" reflect a focus on unity, shared responsibility, and moral resolution. His cirrus cloud and frequency graph show recurring terms such as america, nation, people, and democracy.

Donald Trump Analysis

Trump's speech focuses more on national strength, industry, and promises of action. Common phrases include "we will bring" and "make America," revealing an action-oriented tone. His most used terms include america, people, and country.
